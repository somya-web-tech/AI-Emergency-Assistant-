First Aid Tips

Choking

1. Encourage coughing. 2. Perform the Heimlich maneuver if needed. 3. Call emergency services.

Burns

1. Cool the burn with running water. 2. Cover with a clean cloth. 3. Seek medical help for severe burns.

Heart Attack

1. Call emergency services immediately. 2. Have the person sit or lie down. 3. If trained, perform CPR.
